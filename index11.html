About LionBoyLucky7
Our Platform

LionBoyLucky7 is a digital platform that hosts live Tambola (Housie) games through a custom-coded software system. Both Free and Paid game sessions are available, allowing players to participate in scheduled live draws under a standardized and transparent game process.

How It Works
  1. Register or sign in to your account.
  2. Book or claim your game ticket.
  3. Join the scheduled live Tambola draw.
  4. Match the announced numbers on your ticket.
  5. Complete an eligible winning pattern.
  6. Winning claims are verified before prizes are awarded.

For complete gameplay rules, please refer to the .

Frequently Asked Questions
Is the Free Game completely free?

Yes no entry fee, no payment of any kind. You claim a ticket with a username and your mobile number, one ticket per number, and real prizes are awarded exactly like the paid game.

How are winners verified?

Numbers are called automatically and recorded in real time. A winning pattern is confirmed the instant every number it needs has been called you can follow along yourself on the Live page as it happens.

When are prizes distributed?

Ask your host for the current prize distribution timeline.

What happens if I miss a live draw?

No problem every draw's called numbers and winners are saved and can be reviewed anytime in the Previous Games section.

Can I participate in multiple games?

In the Paid game, yes you can hold more than one ticket. In the Free game, it's one ticket per mobile number, to keep it fair for everyone.
